3. Description of the Service

caila is a private social network that allows you to share and discover real-world plans with your friends. The core features include:

  • Creating and sharing plans: post Events (organised activities), Happenings (personal moments), Trips (travel plans), and Floats (ideas and suggestions).
  • Social connections: add friends, create Crews (private groups), and manage a Close Friends list.
  • City and country profiles: see which friends have visited or lived in a city, and browse their recommendations.
  • World feed and calendar: view friends' upcoming plans in a feed and in a shared calendar view.
  • Scrapbook: photos attached to posts are collected into a personal visual history.
  • Overlaps: automatic matching of your plans, trips, and city visits against friends', surfacing shared connections.
  • Event discovery: browse public events sourced from third-party providers including Ticketmaster.
