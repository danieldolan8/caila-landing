About Caila

A social planning app for real friends.

Caila helps friends share the plans still ahead of them, from trips and events to casual happenings and ideas, so they can spot overlaps before the moment passes.

What Caila is

Caila is a private social app for planning real life with the people already in your life. Instead of looking back at what friends did after it happens, Caila is built around what friends are doing next.

People use Caila to share upcoming trips, events, recurring plans, floats and recommendations with friends or specific crews.

What Caila helps with

  • Seeing when friends will be in the same city.
  • Sharing upcoming trips, events and plans privately.
  • Finding overlaps before they turn into missed chances.
  • Planning with crews instead of losing ideas in group chats.
  • Keeping real-life plans in one calm place.

Why it exists

Group chats are useful, but plans often disappear into old messages. Caila gives friends a clearer way to say where they will be, what they are thinking about doing, and who might want to join.
