Social planning app

Plan trips, events and overlaps with friends.

Caila gives friends a shared place for upcoming plans, so birthdays, trips, runs, gigs, floats and city overlaps do not disappear inside group chats.

For trips

Share where you will be and when. Friends can spot when they are in the same city, country or event window.

For events

Add concerts, festivals, matches, shows and one-off occasions so friends can see what is coming up.

For crews

Plan with the people you actually do things with. Keep crew ideas, polls, calendars and overlaps together.

For ideas

Float a plan before it is fully decided. See who is interested before booking or committing to anything.

How Caila works

Caila is built around future plans rather than past posts. It helps friends move from scattered messages to shared visibility.
